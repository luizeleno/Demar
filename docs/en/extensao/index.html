Culture and community

Undergraduate students regularly participate in extension activities, either through the junior company “EEL Jr”, either through individual participation or in groups of extension and consulting projects. Beginning in 2013, with the beginning of the Professional Master’s Degree in Science Educational Projects, undergraduate students have been supporting the extension activities at the Lorena elementary schools. In addition, some extension activities offered and organized by Demar employees and/or students may be highlighted:

CAEF

Physical Engineering Student Academic Centre

The Academic Center of Physical Engineering (CAEF) of the School of Engineering of Lorena of the University of São Paulo – EEL-USP aims to represent the students of the Physical Engineering course at EEL-USP, to promote the students' approach with the faculty, other students and the technical-administrative staff, organize events or excursions of a scientific, cultural or recreational nature, in addition to ensuring the excellence of the course. For this, in addition to other actions, CAEF provides a space for suggestions, praise or criticism, as well as a reference to freshmen and senior students with several study materials.
